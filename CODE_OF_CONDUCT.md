# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are building a professional, open-source SaaS boilerplate. Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, focus on technical merit, and help maintain a productive environment for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use clear, constructive technical feedback.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Accept maintainers' moderation decisions.
- Report security issues privately through the security policy.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private security reports.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Dismissive or hostile review behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented where appropriate, and focused on preserving a healthy technical community.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a private maintainer communication channel where available or contact the maintainers through GitHub. For security vulnerabilities, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of opening a public issue.
